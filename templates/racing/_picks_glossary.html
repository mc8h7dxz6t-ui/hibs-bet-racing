{# Plain-English guide to picks and percentages — racing cards & place picker. #}
How to read picks & %

Place % — Our estimate of finishing in the places (usually top 3). 42% ≈ a 42-in-100 place chance — not a win guarantee.

Pick / Place Picker — Horses ranked by jockey+trainer form, pace, ratings and model place chance. The summary line explains why that horse leads.

VALUE — Each-way price looks favourable vs our place model and passed safety gates. Not every short-priced favourite is VALUE.

+X% edge — Model place % minus what the place odds imply. +6% means we see about six points more place chance than the price suggests.

Combo — How often this jockey and trainer place together in past runs — a form clue, not a standalone bet.

EV — Expected long-run profit on an each-way bet if the model is accurate. Positive EV is an edge on paper, not a promise.
